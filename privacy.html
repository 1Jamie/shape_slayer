Shape Slayer Privacy & Telemetry Policy

We believe in transparent telemetry. This page explains exactly what gameplay data Shape Slayer collects, how it is used, and how you can opt in or out at any time.

What We Collect

Gameplay Only No Personal Identifiers
  • Run Summary: game mode (solo/multiplayer), run duration, result (success / failure / abandoned), version number, procedural seed.
  • Room Metrics: per-room completion time, damage dealt/taken, hits taken, and room entry/exit stat snapshots (damage, defense, speed, cooldown modifiers, etc.).
  • Boss Encounters: encounter length, player damage to bosses, boss damage to players, phase timing.
  • Gear & Build Data: equipped weapon/armor/accessory, affixes, class modifiers, and key stat multipliers at room start/end.
  • Event Counts: aggregate counts of damage and hit events for balance analysis (no raw controller input or personally identifiable data).

Telemetry is recorded per run. Player IDs used inside telemetry are session identifiers only and do not include personal data.

Why We Collect It

  • Balance & Scaling: track damage/defense curves room-by-room to ensure affixes and enemies scale fairly.
  • Build Diversity: understand which gear combinations are thriving (or underperforming) to guide future updates.
  • Boss & Encounter Tuning: analyze per-phase difficulty spikes and player survivability.
  • Performance Monitoring: detect rooms or affixes that create unexpected spikes in damage or clear times.

When Data Is Sent

  • Telemetry is collected only when the host (or solo player) has opted in.
  • Data is buffered locally during the run and uploaded once the run completes.
  • No telemetry is uploaded if you opt out—collection stops immediately and any buffered run data is discarded.

What We Do With Your Data

Internal Use Only
  • Telemetry is used exclusively by me (the developer) to analyze balance, identify rough edges in progression, and prioritize quality-of-life improvements.
  • Aggregated dashboards guide tuning passes (damage curves, defense scaling, affix power) so every class and build remains viable.
  • Raw telemetry is never shared, sold, or licensed to third parties. There are no ad networks, analytics brokers, or external processors involved.

If I ever introduce community-facing stats (for example, global boss clear rates), they will be published only as anonymized aggregate metrics or I will ask the community for permission and if they want public leaderboards.

What We Don’t Collect

No Tracking IDs No Personal Data
  • No personal identifiers: we never store your name, email, account information, IP address, or hardware fingerprints.
  • No social or network data: telemetry does not include friend lists, chat messages, lobby codes, or network metadata.
  • No cross-run tracking: each run is stored independently and cannot be linked back to previous runs or to a specific player.
  • No raw inputs: we do not record controller/keyboard inputs, screenshots, or video of your gameplay.
  • No monetization data: there are no ads, payment processors, or third-party analytics embedded in Shape Slayer.

All telemetry is anonymous and scoped to a single run. Once a run ends, the data is uploaded (if you opted in) and the local buffer is cleared.

Your Controls

  • You decide during onboarding whether telemetry is enabled.
  • You can revisit the setting anytime from the in-game pause menu (Settings → Privacy).
  • Opting out instantly clears any active telemetry buffer and prevents future uploads until you opt back in.

Contact

Questions or concerns? Reach out via the project issue tracker or contact me directly.
